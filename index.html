The faithful dog

Hachiko

The statue of Hachiko that can be found in Tokyo Shibuya station

Hachiko, the famous akita dog for his loyalty to his owner professor Ueno. Hachiko waited his owner everyday at Shibuya station to come home from work. After two years professor Ueno passed away at work. Even after the death of his owner Hachiko came to the station everyday for roughly nine years. After the long wait Hachiko died at the age of 11 years old and was buried beside his owner.


Hachiko became a symbol of loyalty and is remember to this day and is the inspiration behind many works in popular culture.
